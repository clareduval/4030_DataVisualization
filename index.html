PPP LOAN DISTRIBUTION AND JOB RETENTION

A look in to factors surrounding Paycheck Protection Program loans across the US

PPP loan data, provided to the public by the Small Business Administration, is to provide insight to the driving force of USA's economic stability. This program is essential America's economic rebound, resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic in particular.

PPP loan are funded a lending instiution, then guarenteed by the SBA. The borrower must submit a series of demographics of themselves and their business. Of these, demographics contain amount of jobs retained by approval of the loan and the type of business.
